The McLaren p1 made in 2014 is an engineering marvel! It includes a 3.8-litre twin turbo v8 with 1 electric that makes 903 horsepower and propels the p1 to 60 in 2.5 seconds.

It also has a carbon fiber body and a 7-speed dual-clutch transmission.

The Aston Martin Valkyrie made in 2023 is a race car for the road. It weighs only 2,850 pounds and has 1,160 horsepower. The power comes from a 6.5-litre naturally aspirated v12 made by Cosworth with one electric motor. It does 0-60 in 2.3 seconds and 0-100 in 3.8 seconds.
